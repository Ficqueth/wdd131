What to Plant & Do Each Season

🌸 Spring

  • Start seeds indoors (tomatoes, peppers)
  • Plant leafy greens (lettuce, spinach)
  • Prune shrubs and trees

☀️ Summer

  • Water deeply and mulch
  • Harvest early crops
  • Plant beans, cucumbers, and squash

🍂 Fall

  • Plant garlic and onions
  • Collect leaves for composting
  • Clean up garden beds

❄️ Winter

  • Plan next year’s garden
  • Start an indoor herb garden
  • Clean and sharpen tools
