Site Plan for Gardening Tips & Plant Care

Site Name

Gardening Journey – This name represents my personal journey as a beginner gardener, sharing the experiences and tips learned along the way. The name reflects a warm, approachable theme, aiming to connect with fellow gardeners on a similar path.

Optional Domain Availability: gardeningjourney.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide beginner gardeners with valuable tips, plant care guides, and seasonal gardening calendars. The site will offer a combination of personal insights, plant galleries, and practical advice to help new gardeners grow their plants confidently. The website will serve as a comprehensive resource for learning and troubleshooting gardening tasks.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "What are the best trees to plant for a beginner gardener?"
  • Scenario 2: "How do I create a garden care schedule based on the seasons?"

Color Schema

The color scheme will include two main colors to create a fresh, earthy feel that complements the theme of gardening:

  • Green (#2e8b57): Used for headings, main call-to-action buttons, and accents throughout the website.
  • Tomato Red (#ff6347): Used for highlighting important information, links, and secondary call-to-action buttons.

These colors create a natural, calming feel suitable for a gardening website while also making key elements stand out.

Typography

The typography for the website will include a combination of clean, readable fonts:

  • Headings: Georgia (Serif) – This font will be used for all headings to give a traditional and elegant feel.
  • Body text: Arial (Sans-serif) – The body text will use Arial for better readability and modernity.
  • Quotes/Callouts: Courier New (Monospace) – This font will be used for quotes or highlighted gardening tips, giving them a distinct, focused style.
